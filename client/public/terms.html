Denver Commercial Coatings (DCC) — DCC Project Manager app. Last updated July 2026.

DCC Project Manager is an internal application used by Denver Commercial Coatings to manage painting projects and coordinate with our crews. These terms apply to employees who are given access to the app and to the text message notifications it sends.

Who receives messages

Text notifications are sent only to employees of Denver Commercial Coatings whose phone number has been added to the app by an administrator, for the purpose of receiving work-related updates on projects assigned to them.

Message types and frequency

Messages may include job scheduling updates, project notes, photo or receipt upload alerts, and order updates tied to your assigned projects. Frequency varies with project activity — typically a few messages per week. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to stop receiving text notifications. Reply HELP for help. You may also ask your administrator to remove your phone number from your profile in the app.

No cost to employees

Denver Commercial Coatings does not charge employees for these notifications. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may still apply.
