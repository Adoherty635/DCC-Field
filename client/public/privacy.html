Privacy Policy

Denver Commercial Coatings (DCC) — DCC Project Manager app. Last updated July 2026.

DCC Project Manager is an internal tool used by Denver Commercial Coatings to manage painting projects, schedules, and communication with our crews. This policy explains how we handle information collected through the app, including phone numbers used for text notifications.

Information we collect

The app stores project information, photos, notes, and the names and phone numbers of employees who use the app. Phone numbers are entered by DCC administrators for employees of Denver Commercial Coatings — they are not collected from the general public.

How we use your phone number

Your mobile phone number is used solely to send you text message notifications related to your work, such as job scheduling updates, project notes, and photo or order alerts for projects assigned to you.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your number is used only for the operational purpose described above, and only shared with our SMS provider (Twilio) solely to deliver these messages.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your project activity — typically a few messages per week, and more often during active jobs. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out

You can opt out of text notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by asking your administrator to remove your phone number from your profile in the app. Reply HELP for help.

Data storage

App data, including phone numbers, is stored securely and is only accessible to authenticated DCC employees and administrators through the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to Denver Commercial Coatings at alex@dccpaint.com.
